üìä Key Business Insights
1. Delivery Time-Series Analysis
The Weekend Bottleneck: Discovered that late delivery risk increases by X% on orders placed on Saturdays and Sundays.

Seasonal Spikes: Identified specific months (Q4) where shipping infrastructure is most strained.

Impact: Provided a data-backed recommendation to increase Sunday warehouse staffing to eliminate Monday backlogs.

2. Profitability & "Bleeding" Categories
Identified that specific categories (e.g., Fishing and Fan Shop) frequently generate negative profit despite high sales volume.

Correlated losses with inefficient shipping modes used for low-margin products.

Recommendation: Adjusted shipping pricing strategies for high-volume, low-margin items.

3. Fraud Risk Mitigation
Isolated "Suspected Fraud" patterns using multi-dimensional filtering.

Mapped fraud spikes to specific international shipping routes and customer segments.
